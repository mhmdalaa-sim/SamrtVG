About SmartVG

"Since 2009, Vega Group has been transforming lives with smart home automation, advanced security systems, and cutting-edge technology. As an authorized distributor for Sonoff and Lezn, we deliver trusted solutions tailored to your needs. Beyond technology, we offer premium gift customization services, combining innovation, creativity, and over 15 years of expertise to elevate your lifestyle"

Values

We strive for continuous progress through innovation and quality in every product and service. We uphold integrity, transparency, and professionalism while supporting sustainability by delivering eco-friendly solutions that meet our clients' needs.

Goal

To achieve sustainable growth across all company sectors by expanding our customer base and increasing market share locally and regionally, while solidifying our position as a trusted and innovative provider of technical solutions.

Mission

We are committed to excellence by delivering innovative and high-quality services in technology, software, computers, and gift printing. Through adopting the latest technologies, we aim to enhance customer satisfaction and build long-lasting partnerships.

Vision

We aspire, with a forward-thinking vision, to be the leading company in Egypt and a pioneer in the Middle East in providing smart technology solutions and software. We focus on creativity and excellence to deliver a unique experience that meets our clients' aspirations.
